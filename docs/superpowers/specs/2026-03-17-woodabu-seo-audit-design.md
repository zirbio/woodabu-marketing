## 3. Phase 1: Technical SEO Audit (Weeks 1-2)

### 3.1 Screaming Frog Crawl

Full crawl of woodabu.com (< 500 URLs with 59 products + collections + blog). Identify:

- Pages with duplicate or missing meta title/description
- Broken links (404)
- Redirect chains
- Images without alt text
- Orphan pages (no internal links pointing to them)
- Shopify URL duplication (`/collections/X/products/Y` vs `/products/Y`) — verify canonical tags

### 3.2 Core Web Vitals

Run PageSpeed Insights on 5 key pages:

1. Homepage (`woodabu.com`)
2. Best-seller product page (Cabecero Tulum)
3. High-value collection page (mesas extensibles)
4. Blog index (`/blogs/noticias`)
5. A blog article

**Targets:** LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms, CLS < 0.1

Primary optimization vectors on Shopify: image weight/format (WebP), lazy loading, theme performance.

### 3.3 Schema Markup

Already implemented: FurnitureStore, Organization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList.

Missing (to verify and add):
- **Product** schema with price, availability, and aggregateRating on each product page (enables rich snippets with stars)
- **BlogPosting** schema on each blog article
- **FAQ** schema on the FAQ page
- Validate all with Google Rich Results Test

### 3.4 Indexation & Sitemap

- Review `woodabu.com/sitemap.xml` (auto-generated by Shopify)
- Check Search Console: indexed vs excluded pages, coverage errors
- Ensure out-of-stock product pages redirect or show alternatives (not 404)

### 3.5 On-Page Basics

Audit all 59 product pages + collection pages for:
- Meta titles (many likely auto-generated by Shopify)
- Meta descriptions
- H1 structure (one per page, keyword-inclusive)
- Internal linking between products, collections, and blog

**Deliverable:** Prioritized spreadsheet of all technical findings (critical/high/medium/low) with corrective action for each.

## 5. Phase 3: Keyword Research & On-Page Optimization (Weeks 3-4)

### 5.1 Keyword Clusters

| Cluster | Examples | Intent | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product core | "cabecero madera maciza", "mesa extensible madera" | Transactional | High |
| Product + attribute | "muebles sostenibles España", "muebles artesanales madera" | Transactional | High |
| Product + spec | "cabecero 150 madera", "mesa extensible 6 personas" | Transactional | High |
| Informational/guide | "cómo elegir mesa extensible", "tipos de madera para muebles" | Informational (blog) | Medium |
| Special collections | "muebles reciclados océano", "muebles zero waste" | Niche transactional | Medium |
| Local | "muebles a medida Madrid", "ebanista Madrid" | Local (GBP + landing) | Medium-High |

### 5.2 Search Console Analysis

- Export all queries with impressions > 0
- Identify low-hanging fruit: positions 5-20 with decent impressions (minimal optimization → top 3)
- Identify high-impression/low-CTR pages → rewrite meta titles/descriptions

### 5.3 Competitor Gap Analysis

Using Ubersuggest or Ahrefs Webmaster Tools, find keywords where Hannun, Decowood, LUFE, Woodstock rank but Woodabu doesn't. Filter for intent that matches actual products (ignore "muebles baratos" — not Woodabu's market).

### 5.4 Product & Collection Page Optimization

For each of 59 product pages + collection pages:

- **Meta title:** `{Product} de Madera Maciza {Attribute} | Woodabu` (< 60 chars)
- **Meta description:** 150-160 chars with primary keyword + benefit + CTA
- **H1:** Single H1 with primary keyword
- **Image alt text:** Descriptive with natural keyword
- **Internal links:** Each product → 2-3 related products + relevant blog article

**Deliverable:** Keyword research spreadsheet by cluster + on-page optimization doc with exact meta title/description copy for every page.

## 6. Phase 4: Blog Content Strategy (Weeks 5-8)

### 6.1 Topic Cluster Architecture

| Pillar (2000+ words) | Satellites (800-1200 words) |
|---|---|
| Guía completa de mesas extensibles de madera maciza | Tipos de madera · Cuidar mesa madera · Extensible vs fija · Medidas por comensales |
| Cabeceros de madera: estilos, materiales y cómo elegir | Maciza vs tablero · Para dormitorios pequeños · Tendencias 2026 |
| Muebles sostenibles: qué son y cómo identificarlos | FSC vs PEFC · Greenwashing en muebles · Reciclada vs certificada · Huella de carbono |
| Decorar con madera natural: guía por estancias | Salón · Dormitorio · Recibidor · Combinar madera con otros materiales |

Each pillar links to all its satellites and vice versa, building topical authority.

### 6.2 Optimize Before Creating

The 9 articles from June-July 2025 already cover some topics. Before writing new content:
- Audit each existing article: target keyword, H1, meta description, internal links
- Expand and update articles with potential (e.g., "Guía definitiva para elegir una mesa extensible..." → becomes the pillar)
- Avoid creating duplicates

### 6.3 Editorial Calendar: Sustainable Cadence

Past mistake: 9 articles in one month, then nothing. Google rewards consistency.

- **Target frequency:** 1 article/week (4/month)
- **Monthly mix:** 1 SEO pillar/satellite + 1 brand/storytelling (workshop, processes, Kiko) + 1 seasonal/product + 1 general interest (decor, trends)
- **Seasonal peaks in Spain:** September (back-to-routine, moving), January (sales, resolutions), Spring (renovation). Publish relevant content 6-8 weeks before peak.

### 6.4 Differentiating Content

Assets competitors cannot replicate:
- **Real artisan process** — step-by-step from log to delivery
- **Founder story** — Kiko as forest engineer, grandmother naming, Béjar origin
- **Cause-driven collections** — Pure Oceans (ghost fishing nets), Zero Waste (reclaimed wood) — viral + natural backlink potential
- **Real data** — 300+ reviews, 17K customers, €833 AOV — transparency builds trust

### 6.5 Per-Article SEO Checklist

Every published article must have:
- Clean URL with keyword (`/blogs/noticias/cabeceros-madera-maciza`)
- Meta title with keyword at start (< 60 chars)
- Meta description with keyword + hook (< 160 chars)
- Single H1 with primary keyword
- H2/H3 with secondary/long-tail keywords
- At least 2 internal links to relevant products
- At least 1 internal link to another blog article
- Images with descriptive alt text
- Clear CTA (view product, request quote, contact)

**Deliverable:** Topic cluster map + 8-week editorial calendar + publication checklist + list of existing articles to optimize vs create.

## 7. Measurement & Iteration

### 7.1 KPIs

| KPI | Source | Review Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Organic traffic (sessions) | GA4 | Weekly |
| Search impressions | Search Console | Weekly |
| Average CTR | Search Console | Biweekly |
| Average position | Search Console | Biweekly |
| Keywords in top 10 | Search Console / Ubersuggest | Monthly |
| Conversions from organic | GA4 | Monthly |
| GBP impressions | GBP Insights | Monthly |
| GBP actions (calls, web, directions) | GBP Insights | Monthly |
| Articles published | Blog | Weekly |

**Critical first step:** Extract baseline for ALL KPIs before making any changes.

### 7.2 Monthly Review Cycle

1-hour monthly review:
- Keywords up/down — why?
- Blog articles attracting traffic vs not
- High-impression/low-CTR pages → rewrite meta titles
- New competitors in SERPs?
- Adjust next month's editorial calendar based on data

### 7.3 Automation with This Repo

- **GA4 API** (`src/apis/ga4.ts`): Monthly script to extract organic metrics → `data/insights/`
- **`/analytics` command**: Extend to include SEO-specific metrics
- **Brand guidelines**: All SEO content passes through brand voice filter

### 7.4 Expected Milestones

- **Weeks 1-4:** Technical fixes indexed, GBP optimized, Search Console impressions rising
- **Months 2-3:** Optimized product pages gaining positions, first blog articles indexed
- **Months 4-6:** Topic clusters consolidating, organic traffic trending up, long-tail keywords entering top 10
- **Months 6-12:** Topical authority established, competitive keywords climbing, organic as significant acquisition channel
